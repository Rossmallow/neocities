Photos from around my neighborhood. I wanted to get some more practice and try to be a bit braver when it comes to taking photos of other people.
I have been following <a href="https://www.instagram.com/izzyfts/">izzyfts</a> on Instagram for a bit and really like his style. I got this <a href="https://fujixweekly.com/2020/02/03/my-fujifilm-x-t1-x-trans-ii-kodacolor-film-simulation-recipe/">Kodacolor recipe</a> from Fuji X Weekly (I'll share my slightly-modified settings below) that I think looks like his recipes, and I tried to take some photos in the way he demonstrates in his videos. They don't look great though because I'm still too nervous and not committing enough to the photo. I need to get more comfortable with being uncomfortable. Today was one small step towards that. I'll keep working on it.
